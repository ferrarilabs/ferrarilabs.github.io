How I work

About

Advisory-led, technically grounded.

My work sits at the intersection of compliance, technology, and operational execution. I have spent years helping institutions make better decisions in environments where the cost of being wrong is high: suspicious activity detection, sanctions exposure, onboarding risk, model challenge, and digital asset oversight.

That perspective comes from seeing the same pattern repeatedly. The issue is rarely just the model, or the rule, or the platform. It is usually the gap between what leadership expects, what investigators can actually work with, what the data supports, and what regulators will ultimately challenge.

I also build. Quietly. The reason is simple: some recurring problems are not being solved well enough by legacy approaches. When that happens, advisory alone is not enough.
